Success Stories

Hagana Agro processing company was established and registered in April 2018 based on experiences the founders and the staff inherited from model company (Honey Care Africa) worked in South Sudan from September 2013- March 2018. Just three months after incorporation, Hagana exported 100Kgs of Honey to Japan. To date, we have exported 450kgs of honey to Japan. In total we have bought 11,900kgs of comb honey from farmers, 96.2% of the sourced honey has sold in the local market. Hagana CEO is a proud winner of the 2019 South Sudan Chamber of Commerce Young Entrepreneur Award of the year. Some of the achievements in South Sudan that Hagana is proud about include;
