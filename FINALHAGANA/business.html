OUR MODEL

Haganaâ€™s model is to help South Sudanese farmers receive the required technical and material support to enable them to supply high-quality agricultural produce enough for their own consumption and market surplus. We negotiate a fair price with the farmers, pay each in cash and transport the produce to our production facility in Juba. These are then locally processed, packaged and sold in the South Sudanese market. Any surplus food products are exported. To date, we remain the leading company that is locally sourcing, commercializing using an in-country value addition model to strengthen the local economy and create new jobs.

Linking Smallholder Farmers to Market

Most farmers in South Sudan do not have access to sustainable market due to limited capacity to;

  • Storage of large quantities of farm produce before losing their quality
  • Transportation to access larger markets
  • Value addition to meet the different taste of the consumers in order to create demand
  • Market their produce

Therefore, many farmers sell their surplus in very small quantities at local markets or by the road side exposing their produce to risks of contamination through poor handling, weather conditions and other contaminants like dust. Hagana reduces all these risks through proper storage facilities, bulk procurement, value addition and marketing on behalf of the farmers.
