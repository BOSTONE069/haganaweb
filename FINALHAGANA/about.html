About Us

Hagana Agro Processing Company Ltd is a South Sudanese youth owned company established in early 2018 with the aim of working with rural farming communities of South Sudan to provide them with an additional source of income so that they can have a better and dignified living conditions. 83 percent of South Sudan’s population is rural-based according to the 2008 South Sudan Bureau of statistics censors whose livelihoods depend entirely on subsistence agriculture. Majority of these are the youth and women. Hagana’s primary objectives is to provide guaranteed market as a pull-factor in encouraging these communities to take up commercial farming so that they can contribute towards food security and raise rural incomes. High poverty levels among farming communities in Africa is attributed to lack of guaranteed market as farmers do not get the motivation they need to scale up their farm size beyond what is enough for the stomach. Hagana is a bridge that provides the much needed market so that farmers can produce enough to feed their families and sell any surplus produce to bring in income. We believe our approach will create jobs along the agricultural value chain, enable people across South Sudan to access affordable and nutritious snacks; and diversify the local economy away from oil as we would export any surplus locally grown produce to regional markets.

Vision

To be a leading Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) company in South Sudan producing quality and affordable pure and natural products, utilising a sustainable supply chain that benefits rural farming communities

Mission

To build brand of high quality and affordable honey and honey based snacks that will meet consumer satisfaction, contribute to economic development by increasing rural farming families’ income through sustainable supply chain linkages.

What Hagana Does

Hagana provides capacity building to our partners (farmers) on all aspects of apiculture and apiary management, linking farmers with input suppliers of beekeeping kits to maximize quality and friendliness to bees. Hagana works with farmers by using a geographical cluster system. We provide an essential link between the farmers and the market by adding value to the farm produce to meet different tastes and preference of the vast market. Hagana markets the honey and other honey-based products to create demand essentially increasing the amount of farm produce
